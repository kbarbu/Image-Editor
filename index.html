Scribble

Thin reads like ballpoint pen. Thick reads like a fat crayon held in a fist.

Fewer colors flattens the drawing harder. Very fifth grade.

Low leaves big patches of empty paper and skips the small stuff. High colors in nearly everything, and takes a little longer.

Draws the scribble stroke by stroke in the viewer and records it as a video you can download.

Saves as a transparent PNG. The gaps and odd-shaped holes are part of the look, and every run scribbles differently, so if you don't like one, just run it again.
